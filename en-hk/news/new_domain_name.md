## LIB Provisional Studio's New Milestone: Domain Name Upgrade
### China Standard Time [UTC+8]: September 1, 2024

**In this era of rapid information development, an excellent domain name is crucial for a studio's brand building and online identity. **The new domain name is not only more concise and easy to remember, but also more intuitively reflects our studio's name and brand. Its acquisition marks a further expansion of the provisional studio in the online field and the enhancement of brand influence.**  

**New domain name, new starting point. We will continue to explore and innovate to provide users with richer and more exciting experiences.**  

**We are full of anticipation for the future and are well aware of the great responsibility. LIB Provisional Studio will continue to strive, not only pursuing excellence in technology but also pursuing perfection in service, to meet the needs and expectations of our users. We believe that through our unremitting efforts, LIB Provisional Studio will be able to create a better future together with users.**  

**Thank you for your continuous support and trust. We will continue to move forward and live up to expectations!**
